Bread (Jim Lahey's No-Knead Bread)




Prepare the day ahead.
In a large bowl, combine the flour, yeast, salt and water.
Mix until it all just comes together into a rought dough.
Let it rest covered at room temp for atleast 12 hours, preferably 18.
Remove it onto a floured surface and pull in the edges to the center to create a ball and flip it over.
Let rest covered for 2 more hours.
Half an hour before baking, preheat oven to 450 and put baking vessel inside to heat up.
You can use a dutch oven or pan and metal bowl.
Pull your baking vessel out, put paper at bottom, place the dough inside.
It may look like a mess but that's okay.
Bake covered for 30 minutes, then uncovered for 20.
Cool on a rack.
