Bread

Makes two loafs







Preheat the oven to 350.
In a large bowl, mix the water, sugar yeast.
Add the salt and oil.
Add 5 cups of flour and slowly add the 6th as needed until the dough is just slightly sticky.
Knead for 7 minutes.
Allow to rise for one hour.
Grease two 9x5 inch loaf pans.
Puch dough down and knead for one minute.
Divide in half and place in pans for 30 minutes.
Bake for 30-40 minutes.
Allow to cool for 1-2 hours.
