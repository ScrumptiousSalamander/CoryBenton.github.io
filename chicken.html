Chicken

  • 4 Chicken Breasts (~2.5 lbs)
  • Side rice

Heat a pan lightly coated in oil over medium heat.
Cook for 8 minutes on each side. (165 degrees)

Burnt Hand Sauce

  • 1 cup water or chicken stock
  • 2 tbs corn starch
  • 4 tbs soy sauce
  • 1 tbs lemon juice/white wine vinegar
  • 8 tbs honey
  • Side rice

Bring the cup of water/chicken stock to a simmer. (meduim)
Create a starch slurry with ~1/8 cup water and stir until smooth.
Slowly drizzle in a bit of the starch slurry, don't use the whole thing.
Reduce the heat and add all the other ingredients.

Or you could just use something else like honey bbq or yeri yeri teriyaki sauce.
