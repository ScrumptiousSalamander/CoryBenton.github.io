Chicken


Heat a pan lightly coated in oil over medium heat.
Flicking water onto it, it should sizzle when it's ready.
Place chicken gently into the pan and cook 5-8 minutes each side/white with brown crispness/165 degrees

Rice



Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a covered pot.
Add 1 cup of rice and reduce to a simmer (low) for 20 minutes.
Do not stir or lift the lid during this time.
Take it off the burner an let sit for 5 minutes.
Leftovers last 6 days.
