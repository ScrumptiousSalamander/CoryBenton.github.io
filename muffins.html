Muffins

  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or yogurt?
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup milk

Preheat the oven to 425.
Prepare a 12 count muffin pan with nonstick spray or cupcake liners.
Soften the butter in the microwave.
In a large bowl, mix the butter, brown sugar, eggs, sour cream and vanilla extract.
Add the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon.
Fill each cup or liner.
Bake for about ~22 minutes, adjust by texture of first batch.
