Blueberry Muffins

  • 1 1/2 cups flour(alt 1 3/4 cups)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup milk(alt 3/4 cups)
  • (alt 1/2 cup butter)
  • 1 egg (alt 2 eggs)
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries

Preheat the oven to 400.
Prepare a 12 count muffin pan with nonstick spray or cupcake liners.
In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
Add the oil, egg, milk, and vanilla extract. Don't over mix.
Optionally add the blueberries.
Fill each cup or liner.
Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
