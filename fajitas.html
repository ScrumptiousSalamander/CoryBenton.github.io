Chicken Fajitas













Slice onion and bell peppers into strips.
Heat a pan lightly coated in oil over medium heat.
Saute the bell peppers and onion over medium heat until carmelized.
Take them off the heat.
Put the seasonings in a bowl.
Shread the chicken into that bowl.
Mix the chicken with the seasoning.
Portion out each fajita filling, then roll each into burrito shape.
