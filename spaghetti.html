Spaghetti






Bring a pot of water to a boil
Dice the onion
Defrost the beef in the microwave
Start the noodles boiling in the pot (12 minutes)
Brown the meat over medium while you brown the onion in a different pan (10 minutes, don't burn the onion, deglaze with water if needed)
Drain the noodles. Add meat, sauce, onion, salt, pepper and garlic powder
