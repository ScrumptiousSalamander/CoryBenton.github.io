Teriyaki Chicken

  • 2 lbs chicken breast
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbs honey
  • 5 tsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tbs constarch (for constarch mixture)
  • side rice

Heat a pan lightly coated in oil over medium heat
Cook the chicken breast for 8 minutes on each side
Mix all other ingredients in a saucepan over medium for 1 minute
Mix constarch with 1/4 cup cold water and slowly incorperate into saucepan (5-7 minutes)
Pour over chicken and enjoy
