Meatloaf

  • 2 lbs beef
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 cups oatmeal
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Preheat oven 350
Finely dice the onion
In a large bowl, mix the beef, onion, oatmeal, eggs, milk (water), salt and pepper
Add salt and pepper
Spray a pan with nonstick spray
Form a loaf shape on the pan
Bake for 55 minutes (155 internal temp)

Optional Glaze

  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 4 oz ketchup
  • 1 tbs honey
  • 1 tbs tomato paste
  • 1 tbs apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp worcestershire sauce

Apply to loaf twice, every 15 minutes for the first 30 minutes
