Burgers (Makes 8)


















Combine the beef, cilantro, parsley, garlic, worcentershir sauce, cumin, chili powder, salt and pepper and mix by hand.
Seperate out into 8 flat disks. Make them extra wide because they will shrink.
Cook on medium, flipping after 2-3 minutes and adding cheese, then placing on bun after another 2-3 minutes.
Prepare the other ingredients.
Stack order: patty, cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, condiments.
