Spicy Tofu














Make rice.
Cube tofu.
Boil the tofu over medium heat for 10 minutes.
In a pan, cook up the pork in a few tablespoons of oil until brown and part of it is crispy.
Cut the garlic and red stuff into small pieces.
Don't drain, push the pork to the side, turn off the heat, put in the red stuff in the oil and and saute for about a minute. Turn the heat back on, add garlic and chili powder, combine into a paste.
Drain tofu.
Add 2 cups of water, tofu, chicken bouillon, soy sauce, rice vinegar, rice wine, and MSG.
Simmer on medium high for 10 minutes.
Add the sichuan peppercorns, corn starch water solution and sesame oil and enjoy.
