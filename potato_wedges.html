Potato Wedge Recipe

  • 3 large russet potatoes
  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • 1 tbs garlic powder
  • 1 tbs dried oregano
  • 1 tbs paprika
  • ½ cup parmesan

Preheat oven to 400. Thoroughly wash potatoes, and cut into 8ths. Toss wedges in seasoning. Place on a baking sheet, skin side down, and bake for 40-50 minutes.
