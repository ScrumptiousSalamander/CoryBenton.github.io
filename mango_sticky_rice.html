Mango Sticky Rice





Wash rice until the water is clear.
Soak rice in cold water overnight.
Drain rice.
Steam rice.
In a small saucepan, bring 1 cup of coconut milk to a boil.
Add 1/3 cup sugar and 1/4 teaspoon salt, stir until dissolved.
Remove from heat but keep warm.
Transfer cooked rice to a bowl and stir in coconut-milk mixutre.
Let rice stand, covered, 30 minutes, or until coconut-milk mixture is absorbed.
Boil remaining 1/3 cup coconut milk with remaining 3 tbs sugar, stirring for 1 minute.
Serve mango cut into slices and sticky rice drizzled with sauce.
