Pan Lasagna

  • 10 sheets of lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb italian sausage
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 28 oz can of crushed san marzano tomatoes
  • 8 oz can tomato sauce
  • Optionally 1 cup each mozzarella and ricotta
  • Side of green beans

Break the 10 sheets of lasagna into squares.
Dice the onion.
Decase the italian sausage.
Heat a table spoon of olive oil over medium high heat until shimmering.
Add the italian sausage. Break into pieces and cook until brown.
Remove from pan by scooping onto paper towel, leaving oil in the pan.
Saute the onion in the oil for 3-5 minutes until translucent.
Add 2 crushed cloves of garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
Add the can of tomatoes, drained sausage and 1/2 cup hot water.
Bring to a simmer. (medium-low)
Add the broken lasagna noodles and fold them in.
Top with the can of tomato sauce.
Bring back up to a simmer.
Cover and cook for 20 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes to avoid sticking.
Add optional cheese.
