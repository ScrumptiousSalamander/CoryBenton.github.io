Pan Lasagna









Break the 9 sheets of lasagna into squares.
Dice the onion.
Decase the italian sausage.
Heat a table spoon of olive oil over medium high heat until shimmering.
Add the italian sausage. Break into pieces and cook until brown.
Remove from pan by scooping onto paper towel, leaving oil in the pan.
Saute the onion in the oil for 3-5 minutes until translucent.
Add the can of tomatoes, sausage, garlic powder and 1/2 cup hot water.
Bring to a simmer. (medium-low)
Add the broken lasagna noodles and fold them in.
Top with the can of tomato sauce.
Bring back up to a simmer.
Cover and cook for 30? minutes, stirring the bottom every 5 minutes to avoid burning!
Add optional cheese.
