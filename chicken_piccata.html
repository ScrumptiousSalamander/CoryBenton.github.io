Chicken Piccata







Cook rice.
Cut each chicken breast in half and pound thin ~1/4 inch.
Season all chicken with salt and pepper.
Dredge all chicken in flour.
Melt 2 tablespoons of butter with 3 tablespoons olive oil over medium high heat.
Dredge 2-3 slices of chicken in flour again.
When butter and oil start to sizzle, add the chicken and cook for 3-4 minutes per side.
Remove and repeat steps 4-6.
For the sauce, bring to a boil the lemon juice, stock, butter, and capers including the caper juice.
Check for seasoning.
Serve.
