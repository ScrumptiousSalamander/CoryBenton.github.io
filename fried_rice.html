Fried Rice







If you're making the same day, open the rice cooker and let dry out untouched for 2 hours
Precook the cubed chicken/steak and eggs
Warm up the vegitables in the microwave
In a wide pan melt the butter over medium
Mix in the rice and soy sauce
Press into a layer to let fry 8-10 minutes, checking and ocationally stiring to prevent burning
Do not go over 10 minutes! It can get hard without turning brown
Add the meat and vegies to warm up and serve
