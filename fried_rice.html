Fried Rice

  • 3 tbs butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups leftover rice
  • 1 package frozen vegetables (peas, diced carrots, corn, green beans)
  • 3-4 tbs soy sauce
  • Optional thick ham

Optionally slice the ham into cubes.
In a large pan, heat butter and then scramble 2 eggs over medium-high heat.
Add rice, frozen vegetables, soy sauce and ham.
Cook until the rice starts turning slightly brown. (3 minutes)
