Fried Rice

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups leftover rice
  • 1 package frozen vegetables (peas, diced carrots, corn, green beans)
  • 3-4 tbs soy sauce
  • thick ham

Slice thick ham into cubes
In a large pan, heat butter and then scramble 2 eggs over medium-high heat
Add rice, frozen vegetables, ham and soy sauce
