Chicken Enchiladas






Preheat oven to 350.
Finely chop the onion.
In a bowl, shread the chicken.
Add the onion, beans, and half the sauce.
Warm the tortillas in the microwave for 1 minutes.
Lightly oil a casserole dish.
Optionally cover your tortillas in sauce at this stage.
Divide filling evenly among tortillas.
Roll enchiladas and place seam side down in the casserole dish.
Pour the remaining sauce on top.
Bake for 20 minutes.
