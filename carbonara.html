Carbonara








Bring a pot of water to a boil.
Cook the spaghetti for 10-12 minutes.
Drain *SAVE 1/4 CUP PASTA WATER*
Whisk 2 eggs while slowly adding the pasta water.
Cut the bell peppers into half strips.
Cook the bell peppers over medium heat.
Cut half the chicken into small pieces.
Combine everything.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
