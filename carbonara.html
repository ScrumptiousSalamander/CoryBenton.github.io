Carbonara









Preheat oven to 375.
Bring a pot of water to a boil with a little salt and oil.
Cook the spaghetti for 12 minutes, drain and set aside.
Bake bacon for 15-20 minutes.
Cut the bell peppers into half strips.
Cook the bell peppers in bacon grease over medium heat.
In the noodle pot, make the sauce with the half and half, egg yolks, parm.
Cut the chicken and bacon into small pieces.
Combine everything.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
