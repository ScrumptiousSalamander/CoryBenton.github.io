Carbonara





Preheat oven to 350 and start a pot of boiling water.
Cut 2 bell peppers into strips.
Cook pasta as it says on box (6 min?)
Drain (except ~1/4 cup of pasta water) and add 3 eggs and parm cheese and stir occasionally until emulsified.
Bake bacon for 10 minutes then cut into pieces and put greese into a pan.
Cook the bell peppers in the bacon greese.
Combine everything and add salt.
