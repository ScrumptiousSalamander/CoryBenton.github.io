Rice Crispy Treats

  • 6 cups rice crispies
  • 1 10 oz package marshmallows OR 5 1/2 cups mini marshmallows
  • 3 tbs butter

In a large pan, melt butter over low heat.
Add the marshmallows and stir until completely melted.
Remove from heat.
Add rice crispies.
Stir until well coated.
Spray a 13/9 pan with cooking spray.
Press mixture into pan and allow to cool.
Cut into 2 inch squares and enjoy.
