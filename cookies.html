Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • 1 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup white suger
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 1Â½ cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cups quick oats
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Preheat the oven to 375.
Melt the butter in the microwave, but don't get it sizzling hot.
In a large bowl, mix the butter, sugar, vanilla, and eggs.
Add the flour, baking soda, and salt.
Slowly stir in the oats and chocolate chips.
Bake for 8-10 minutes.
