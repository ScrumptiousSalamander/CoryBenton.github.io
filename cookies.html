Cookie Recipe

Preheat the oven to 375. Mix ingredients in the order listed. Bake for 9-12 minutes

  • 1 cup butter (melted)
  • 2 cups white sugar and a dalup of molasis
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups flour (or substitute half a cup with instant oats)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
