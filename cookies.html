Chocolate Chip Cookies












Preheat the oven to 375.
Melt the butter in the microwave until just melted.
In a large bowl, mix the butter, sugar, molasses, vanilla, and eggs.
Add the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
Slowly stir in the oats and chocolate chips.
Roll into slightly larger than golf balls and spread across a baking sheet.
Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
Let the cookies sit on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a plate to cool completely.
