Chicken Chimichangas









Preheat oven to 350.
Finely chop the onion.
In a bowl, shread the chicken.
Add the onion, beans, and taco sauce.
Warm the tortillas in the microwave for 1 minutes.
Spray 9x13 baking pan.
Divide filling evenly among tortillas.
Wrap your chimichangas like a burrito and place seam side down on the pan.
Bake for 30 minutes, flipping half way through.
