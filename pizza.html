Pizza Recipe

Personal 4 Small 4 Large
Water ¾ Cups 2 ¼ Cups 3 Cups
Flour 2 Cups 6 Cups 8 Cups
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tbs salt

Preheat the oven to 425. Proof yeast in water and sugar, it should bloom after 5 minutes. Mix into a rough dough and kneed into a ball. Let sit to autolyse for 20 minutes. Bake for 15 minutes, add cheese after 10.
