Pizza
1 Pizza 3 Pizzas
Flour 2 Cups 6 Cups
Water 3/4 Cups 2 1/4 Cups

Also add a tsp yeast, sugar, salt, oil
Easy 2 Topping Pizza:

  • Pepperoni
  • Sausage

Supreme Toppings:

  • Pepperoni
  • Onion
  • Green Bell Papper
  • Mushrooms
  • Olives

Hawaiian Toppings

  • Pineapple
  • Thick Ham Sliced into small pieces

Preheat the oven to 500.
Mix the dough into a ball, let sit for 20 minutes to autolyse.
Roll out onto a pan.
Add sauce and other ingredients (except cheese.)
To avoid cheese-oil seperation, add cheese ~5 minutes before it finishes.
Cooktime depends on the thickness of the crust:
~1/4 inch ~12 minutes
~1/2 inch ~20 minutes
