Pizza

  • Optionally mozzarella (string cheese)

Dough

  • (Single pizza, should double for 2 though)
  • 2 cups bread flour
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tbs salt

Supreme Toppings

  • 1 package pepperoni
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 small can mushrooms
  • 1 small can olives

Hawaiian Toppings

  • 10 slices of ham
  • 1 small can pinapple chunks

Preheat the oven to 425.
Proof yeast in water and sugar, it should bloom after 5 minutes.
Mix into a rough dough and kneed into a ball.
For larger quantities, seperate into equal portions and refridgerate in well oiled containers.
Let sit to autolyse for 20 minutes.
Roll out onto baking pan.
Optionally, poke with fork to discourage air pockets forming.
Add sauce and ingredients other than cheese.
Bake for 15 minutes, add cheese after 10 minutes.
