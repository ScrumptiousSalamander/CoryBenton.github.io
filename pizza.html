Pizza

Classic toppings:

  • Pepperoni
  • Sausage

For supreme add:

  • Onion
  • Green Bell Papper
  • Mushrooms
  • Olives

Hawaiian toppings:

1 Pizza 3 Pizzas
Flour 2 Cups 6 Cups
Water 3/4 Cups 2 1/4 Cups
Also, add yeast, sugar, salt and oil

Preheat oven to 500.
Mix ingredients into a sticky dough and rollout onto pan.
In order, top with: sauce, brown sugar, pepperoni, then other toppings except cheese.
Add cheese for just the last minute.
For thin crust ~1/4" cook 12-15 minutes.
for thick crust ~1/2" cook 17-20 minutes.
