Pizza

Classic:

  • Pepperoni
  • Sausage

Supreme:

  • Pepperoni
  • Onion
  • Green Bell Papper
  • Mushrooms
  • Olives

Hawaiian:

  • Pineapple
  • Thick Ham Sliced into small pieces
1 Pizza 3 Pizzas
Flour 2 Cups 6 Cups
Water 3/4 Cups 2 1/4 Cups
Also, add yeast, sugar, salt and oil

Preheat oven to 500.
Mix the dough into a ball, let sit for 20 minutes to autolyse.
If dough hasn't had atleast a day to rise, roll it out slightly thicker. If it has, slightly thinner.
Add sauce, a tiny bit of brown sugar, then the other ingredients except cheese.
For thinner crust (~1/4 inch) cook for 12-15 minutes, for thicker crust (~1/2 inch) cook for 17-20 minutes.
Add cheese during the last minute.
