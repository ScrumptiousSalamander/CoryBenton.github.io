Currently, I roll out the dough and cook at 500 for 7 minutes to avoid doughiness.
Proof yeast in warm water and sugar, it should bloom after 5 minutes.
While that's blooming, prep your toppings.
Mix into a rough dough and kneed into a ball.
For larger quantities, seperate into equal portions and refridgerate in well oiled containers.
Let sit to autolyse for 20 minutes.
Roll out onto a baking pan, making sure the middle is thin so it cooks through.
Add sauce and ingredients other than cheese.
Bake for 20-25 minutes, optionally add cheese during the last 5 minutes.
