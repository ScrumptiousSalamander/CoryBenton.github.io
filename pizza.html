Pizza Recipe

Size Personal 4 Small 4 Large
Water ¾ Cups 2 ¼ Cups 3 Cups
Flour 2 Cups 6 Cups 8 Cups
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tbs salt

Preheat the oven to 425.
Proof yeast in water and sugar, it should bloom after 5 minutes.
Mix into a rough dough and kneed into a ball.
For larger quantities, seperate into four equal portions and refridgerate the other three.
Let sit to autolyse for 20 minutes.
Roll out onto baking pan.
Optionally, poke with fork to discourage air pockets forming.
Add sauce and ingredients other than cheese.
Bake for 15 minutes, add cheese after 10 minutes.

Sauce

  • 14 oz can San Marzano tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil

Blend on medium speed for 15-30 seconds until smooth with a little texture.
