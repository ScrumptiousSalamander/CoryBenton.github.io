Hard Boiled Egg Sandwich

Bring a pot of water to a boil. (medium high)
Submerge 6 eggs and cook for 12 minutes.
Remove the eggs and shock in ice water for easy peeling.
Peel and finely chop/grate the eggs.
In a bowl, combine the eggs with 3 1/2 tbs mayonnaise and optionally 1 tsp dijon mustard.
Evenly distribute filling among 3 sandwiches.

Tuna Sandwich

Strain can of tuna.
Mix tuna with 1/2 the can of mayo and 1/4 the can of relish.
Optionally assemble with a bed of lettuce.
Optionally toast the bread.

French Dip Sandwich







Bring 1 cup of beef broth to a boil then reduce to a simmer (low) until sandwich is ready.
Slice the bell pepper into strips.
In a pan, roast the bell pepper with crushed garlic. Remove from pan.
Add mozzarella and bell pepper to each sandwich.
With tongs, soak each piece of roast beef in the broth and add to each sandwich.
Pour beef broth in fridge safe container and use for dipping.
