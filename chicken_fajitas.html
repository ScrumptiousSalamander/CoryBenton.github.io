Chicken Fajitas

  • 20 flour tortillas
  • 2 lbs chicken breast
  • 3 bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tbs chili powder
  • 1 tbs ground cumin
  • 1 tbs garlic powder
  • 1 tbs paprika
  • 1 tbs oregano
  • side black beans and rice

Slice onion and bell peppers into strips
Heat a pan lightly coated in oil over medium heat
Generously coat the chicken in seasonings, plus salt and pepper
Saute the bell peppers and onion over medium heat until carmelized
Cook each breast for 8 minutes on each side
Slice each breast into strips
Assemble fajitas
