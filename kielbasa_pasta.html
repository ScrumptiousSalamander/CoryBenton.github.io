Kielbasa Pasta










Cut kielbasa into disks.
Cook over medium for a few minutes.
Dice half an onion.
Cook the onion.
Add a heaping tablespoon full of garlic for a minute.
Deglase with chicken broth, whipping cream and tomato sauce.
Season with morton season all.
Add a half box of pasta.
Lid and cook for 10 minutes? maybe 20?
Add cheese, garnish with parsley and serve.
