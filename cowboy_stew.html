Cowboy Stew











Wash and dice the potato.
Dice the onion.
Cook the bacon over medium-high heat for 5-6 minutes each side.
Remove the bacon, lower the tempurature to medium and add the onion for 5-7 minutes.<
While that's cooking, cut the kielbasa into disks.
Open all the cans.
Drain the corn but not the beans.
Add the kielbasa, diced tomatoes, beans, corn, and potato.
Stir.
Add chili powder, garlic powder and 1/2 cup water.
Bring to a boil then immediately reduce to simmer (low).
Cover and let simmer on low for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
