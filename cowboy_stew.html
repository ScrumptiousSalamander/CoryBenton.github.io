Cowboy Stew













Wash and dice the potato.
Dice the onion.
In a lightly oiled pot, sautee the onion over medium heat 5-7 minutes.
While that's cooking, cut the sausage into disks.
Remove the onion and replace with the sausage.
Brown both sides.
While that's browning, open all the cans.
Drain the corn but not the beans.
Add diced tomatoes, beans, corn, and potato.
Stir.
Add chili powder, garlic powder and 1/2 cup water.
Bring to a boil then immediately reduce to simmer (low).
Cover and let simmer on low for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
