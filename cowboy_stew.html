Cowboy Stew

  • 1 12 oz package of kielbasa/chorizo? sausage
  • 1 lb frozen ground beef?
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 small can 8oz? crushed tomatoes
  • 1 16 oz can baked beans
  • 1 small can 8oz? whole kernel sweet corn
  • 1 medium russet potato
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 or 1/2 cup water

Wash and dice the potato.
Dice the onion.
In a pot, sautee over medium heat 5-7 minutes.
While that's cooking, cut the sausage into disks.
Remove the onion and replace with the sausage.
Brown both sides.
 Beef??
 Remove the sausage.
 Lightly oil the pot? or has the sausage oiled it?
 Cook the beef over medium high heat for 7-10 minutes until brown.
Add crushed garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
Pour in diced tomatoes, beans, corn, and potato.
Stir.
Add chili powder.
Pour in 1/2 cup water.
Bring to a boil then immediately reduce to simmer.
Let simmer, covered, for 1 hour.
