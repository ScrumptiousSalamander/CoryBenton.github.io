Broccoli Beef








Difficulty: 4/5
Cook rice. Rice
Cut broccoli into bite size pieces.
Cut beef against the grain as thin as you can and about 1 inch wide (bite size).
In a lightly oiled pan, fry the broccoli on medium high for 2-3 minutes, stirring to prevent burning.
Then reduce to medium, add 1 cup of water, lid and let cook for 5 minutes.
Broccoli is finished when it's easily pierced with a fork.
Strain the broccoli.
Cook the beef in a medium high pan for ~1 minute each side. The second side cooks faster.
IMPORTANT: If you see steam/smoke and your pan isnt filling with juices REDUCE HEAT IMMEDIATELY!
That means you're buringing the steak and it will taste burnt.
Drain the beef.
Reintroduce broccoli.
Either add 1/3 cup water and the ginger, soy sauce and sugar, or use 4 tbs teriyaki sauce.
Enjoy.
