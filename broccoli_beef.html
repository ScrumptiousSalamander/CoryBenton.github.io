Broccoli Beef






Night before: Cut the beef against the grain very thin and marinate in 1/2 teriyaki sauce 1/2 lemon juice.
Day of: Start the rice.
Cut broccoli into bite size pieces.
Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium high, fry the broccoli for 2-3 minutes, stir to prevent burning.
Heat another pan over medium high (no oil) and add one and a half handfulls of beef.
Reduce the broccoli to medium, add 1 cup of water, lid and let cook for another ~5 minutes until easily pierced by a fork.
Stir the beef until browned, it will release liquid, remove from heat before the liquid completely evaporates.
Repeat for beef until completely cooked.
Cover beef in more terriaki sauce.
