Italian Sausage and Bow Ties

  • 1 16 oz pack of bow tie pasta
  • 1 lbs italian sausage
  • 1 can red sauce

Cook pasta as it says on box. Drain.
Slice itanian sausage into disks and cook over medium heat until brown (5-7 minutes)
Stir together with pasta and sauce.
