Pork Cutlet







Remove any bones from the porklets.
Pound the porklets into 1/8 inch flats.
Marinade the porklets in Tonkatsu Sauce, or the usual lemon juice, worcestershire, soy, brown sugar, mustard, garlic.
Cover the porklets in salt and pepper, then flour, then egg, then panco.
Heat a pan of oil over medium heat for a few minutes.
Cook the porklets for ~5 minutes on each side. Panco burns quickly, so check every minute!
