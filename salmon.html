Salmon










Preheat oven to 425. Wash asparagus. Bend asparagus, it will snap where it starts to get woody. Throw the hard part away. Lightly season with 8 shakes of salt, 4 shakes of pepper, 2 shakes of garlic powder and a bunch of lemon juice. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes. Take asparagus out. Reduce oven to 375. Wash the salmon and get rid of any bones the butcher missed. Place salmon skin side down on oiled foil on baking sheet. Blend seasonings until it's 1 tbs spice blend for every 1/2 pound of salmon. (or just coat that shit) Season the salmon. Bake 10-40 minutes until the top is no longer goey and a fork inserted and twisted pulls up flakes of salmon.
