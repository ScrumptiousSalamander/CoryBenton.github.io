Ingredients

Prehead oven to 375. Mix all the wet ingredients, then mix in all of the dry ones. Bake for 9 minutes, then take them out immediately. They won't look done but they'll finish cooking on the baking sheet. Makes about 44 cookies if you're using golf ball sized scoops. Optionally, you can add a dollop of molasses and/or a teaspoon of vanilla, I can't taste a difference. Additionally you could swap out cup of flour for two cups of instant oats if you're going for more of a snack than a dessert.

Ingredient Quantity
Butter 1 Cup
Sugar 2 Cups
Eggs 2
Flour 3 Cups
Baking Soda 1 tsp
Baking Powder 1/2 tsp
Chocolate Chips 2 Cups
