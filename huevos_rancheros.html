Huevos Rancheros

  • 1 tortilla
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 egg
  • 1 jar herdez salsa casera
  • Optional shredded cheddar cheese

Heat a pan lightly coated in oil over medium heat (~5 minutes)
Crack the egg into the pan cover until the whites are set. (~2 minutes)
While the egg is cooking, assemble on a plate a tortilla covered in heated black beans.
Optionally top the beans with cheddar cheese.
Slide the egg out of the skillet onto the beans and place the salsa on the side.
