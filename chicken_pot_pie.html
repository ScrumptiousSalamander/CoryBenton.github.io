Chicken Pot Pie










Preheat oven to 400.
Cube your chicken and chop your veggies.
Melt half the butter in 10 inch cast iron skillet.
Add chicken and cook for 2-3 minutes.
It's not finished but remove the chicken from pan.
Add the other half of the butter in the pan.
Immediately add onion for 2 minutes.
Add other chopped vegies for 5 minutes.
Add 4 tbs flour, mix.
Add chicken broth slowly, stirring continuously.
Reintroduce the chicken and cook for 5 minutes.
Add salt and pepper.
Add peas.
Optionally cover with bread crumbs.
Bake 10/20/30 minutes.
