Chicken Pot Pie









Preheat the oven to 425.
Unfurl the pie crusts, one onto a 9 inch glass pie pan and the other onto a baking sheet, and poke holes in them!
Melt butter over medium heat.
Stir in flour, salt and pepper until well blended.
Gradually stir in broth and milk, cooking and stirring until bubbly and thickened.
Stir in chicken and mixed vegetables, then remove from heat.
Bake the pie crusts for ~20 minutes, maybe take top crust out after 15.
Take them out, they should be mostly done.
Fill with filling, make sure top crust is touching the filling so it doesn't dry out.
Bake for an additional ~10 minutes.
Let cool 5 minutes before serving.
