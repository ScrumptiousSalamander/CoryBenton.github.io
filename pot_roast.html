Pot Roast

  • 3-5 lbs eye roast
  • 5 potatoes
  • 6-8 whole carrots
  • celery
  • onion
  • 3 cups beef broth

Eye roast is cheap yet delicious, but it is a little tough so optionally dry brine.
Dry brining involves coating in a thin layer of salt and refridgerating uncovered for 1-2 days.
Preheat oven to 275.
Generously salt and pepper the roast (if you didn't dry brine)
Optionally, if your roast has a fat cap you can score it to help it render out.
Sear the meat in oil over medium-hot until browned on each side.
Place the roast in dutch oven and cover in vegetables and broth.
Put lid on and roast for 3-4 hours (when the roast is fall apart tender).
