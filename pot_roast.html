Pot Roast

  • 3-5 lbs chuck roast
  • 5 potatoes
  • 6-8 whole carrots
  • celery
  • onion
  • 3 cups beef broth

Preheat oven to 275
Generously salt and pepper chuck roast
Sear the meat in oil over medium-hot until browned on each side
Place chuck in dutch oven and cover in vegetables and broth
Put lid on and roast for 3-4 hours (when the roast is fall apart tender)
